Over the past year, [FedRAMP](https://www.fedramp.gov/) gathered feedback from CSPs and JAB review teams on how we could streamline, clarify, and improve the Continuous Monitoring (ConMon) Process. As a result, weâ€™ve made updates to existing documents and created new documents to:
