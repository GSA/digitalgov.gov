During the three months, 141 million people owned smartphones.

Top Smartphone Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs)

  *  Apple &#8211; 39.2%
  * Samsung &#8211; 23.0%
  * HTC &#8211; 8.7%
  * Motorola &#8211; 7.8%
  * LG &#8211; 6.7%

Top Smartphone Platforms

  * Android &#8211; 52.4%
  * Apple &#8211; 39.2%
  * Blackberry &#8211; 4.8%
  * Microsoft &#8211; 3.0%
  * Symbian &#8211; 0.4%
